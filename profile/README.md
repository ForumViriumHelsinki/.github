# Forum Virium Helsinki

Forum Virium Helsinki is a Helsinki city development company driving innovation in urban services, smart city solutions, and open data.

We build open-source tools for urban mobility, IoT data platforms, real-time public transport, and civic technology.

## What we build

- **IoT & Data Platforms** — Sensor networks, data pipelines, and open APIs for urban data
- **Smart City Services** — Mobility tools, environmental monitoring, and city infrastructure integrations
- **Open Source** — Our projects are public and open for contribution

## Technology

We work primarily with Python, TypeScript/Node.js, and Kubernetes on Google Cloud Platform. Our infrastructure is managed with Terraform and deployed via ArgoCD.
